Sushi Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Sushi rice
  • Assorted fresh fish and vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Place a sheet of plastic wrap on a sushi rolling mat.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on the plastic wrap, shiny side down.
  3. Wet your hands and grab a handful of sushi rice. Spread it evenly on the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  4. Add your choice of fish and vegetables on top of the rice.
  5. Roll the nori tightly, using the mat to shape it into a cylinder.
  6. Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
