Chicken Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the cooked chicken with taco seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat the flour tortillas in a dry skillet until warm.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing a portion of seasoned chicken on each tortilla.
  4. Top with diced tomatoes, onions, shredded lettuce, and chopped cilantro.
  5. Drizzle with sour cream and sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Serve and enjoy your delicious chicken tacos!
