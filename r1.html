Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and Parmesan cheese. Season with black pepper.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the egg and cheese mixture, then add the crispy pancetta or guanciale.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper.

Nutritional Information

Protein: 20g
Carbohydrates: 35g
