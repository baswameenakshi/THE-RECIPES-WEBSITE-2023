Spinach Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the fresh spinach, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
  3. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve your delicious spinach salad immediately!

Nutritional Information

Calories: 150 kcal per serving
Protein: 4g
Carbohydrates: 12g
Fat: 9g

Vitamins

Vitamin A: 2500 IU
Vitamin C: 30 mg
Vitamin K: 200 mcg
