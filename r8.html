Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan and saut√© the onions until they become translucent.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned.
  4. Stir in the curry powder and cook for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add the tomatoes and coconut milk, and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot with rice or naan.
