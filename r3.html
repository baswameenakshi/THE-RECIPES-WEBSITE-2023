Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Top with pepperoni, black olives, green bell pepper, mushrooms, onions, and tomatoes.
  7. Season with dried oregano and crushed red pepper flakes.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Slice and serve your delicious pizza!
