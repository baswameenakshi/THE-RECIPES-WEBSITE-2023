Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
  2. Season the patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare or longer for your desired doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add cheese slices on top of the patties, if desired.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  7. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and condiments on the toasted buns.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade burger!
